About

Shashwat Kakkar, CFA

I am a finance professional and CFA charterholder with hands-on experience in corporate finance, debt restructuring, and banking relationships at a manufacturing business I help run. My work at the intersection of operations and structured finance has given me a grounded, first-principles understanding of how capital is deployed and how businesses actually function — beyond the spreadsheet.

Obsidian Capital Research is my independent research platform: a growing portfolio of deep-dive equity analysis focused on real assets, infrastructure, and listed real estate. Every report is authored to institutional standards — transparent assumptions, rigorous methodology, and a clear, defensible investment thesis.

My long-term focus is on real and alternative assets in high-growth markets, particularly the UAE and broader Asia. I am currently seeking an analyst role at an investment institution where I can contribute meaningfully and continue building expertise in this space.
