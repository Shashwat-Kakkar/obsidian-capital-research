Investment Thesis

Embassy Office Parks REIT is India's largest office REIT by leasable area, with a portfolio of premium Grade-A assets concentrated in Bengaluru, Mumbai, Pune, and NCR. The trust offers a compelling combination of stable, contracted rental income with built-in escalation clauses, a high-quality tenant base (40%+ Fortune 500 occupiers), and meaningful exposure to India's structurally growing commercial real estate market.

Our analysis identifies the current unit price as trading at a modest discount to a conservatively estimated NAV, supported by robust FFO generation and a sound debt coverage profile. Key risks — including tenant concentration, interest rate sensitivity on variable-rate debt, and macroeconomic headwinds to IT/ITES sector demand — are explicitly modelled and stress-tested.

The full report details our assumptions, methodology, and scenario analysis, and the accompanying Excel model is fully transparent and editable. Disagreement with any assumption is a feature, not a bug.
